Radisson Blu Kochi

This stylish hotel,Radisson Blu Kochi boasts 5 dining options and a rooftop swimming pool. Located in the heart of Ernakulam City, it houses a gym and spa. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Offering a modern décor, air-conditioned rooms at Radisson Blu Kochi feature a flat-screen TV and a personal safe. A minibar and coffee/tea making facilities are included. Room service is available round-the-clock.

disson Blu is a 45-minute drive from Cochin International Airport and an hour’s drive from Cherai Beach.

Keshia Restaurant, which is also a 24-hour coffee shop offers buffet and á la carte specialities while Zhima serving Oriental cuisine offers authentic Chinese cuisines. Overlooking Cochin, the rooftop High Bar features cocktails and barbecued meals with Mediterranean dishes.

Guests can soak in a steam bath or enjoy a spa treatment. Alternatively, they can exercise in the gym, which also offers personal training sessions. Other facilities include a business centre and a tour desk.
